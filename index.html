Self Care in the Mind

Mental health includes emotional, psychological, and social well-being. It is more than the absence of a mental illness—it’s essential to your overall health and quality of life. Self-care can play a role in maintaining your mental health and help support your treatment and recovery if you have a mental illness. Self-care means taking the time to do things that help you live well and improve both your physical health and mental health. This can help you manage stress, lower your risk of illness, and increase your energy. Even small acts of self-care in your daily life can have a big impact.

We Are Here for you!

If you or a loved one are struggling with your mental health, we provide services and helplines to help you go through a proper treatment. We welcome you to our programs, workshops, and support groups. You are not alone in this, together we can help you become a healthier person.
