About Us

Our Mission

MindCare Initiative is dedicated to promoting mental health awareness, providing support and resources for individuals struggling with mental health issues, and advocating for improved mental health care policies. Through education and policy advocacy, we aim to create a more inclusive and supportive environment for all.

Our Vision

We envision a society where mental health is prioritized, and every individual has access to the support and resources they need to achieve mental wellness. Additionally, we advocate for continuous education and awareness to reduce stigma and empower individuals in their journey towards emotional well-being.

Meet Our Team

Dr. Sam Seplow, DO

Chief Medical Officer Psychiatrist

Dr. Seplow earned his medical degree from Des Moines University. He completed a year-long internship in general medicine and a four-year residency in general psychiatry. He has been practicing clinical psychiatry for almost 20 years. At Complete Mind Care, Dr. Seplow wears many hats, with administrative and supervisory roles as well as ongoing clinical work. He is board certified in general psychiatry by the American Board of Psychiatry and Neurology.

Christina Villani

Director of Clinical Services, Dr. of Clinical Psychology

Dr. Villani is a graduate of Lehigh University, where she earned her Bachelor of Arts in Psychology and Sociology. She earned her Doctor of Psychology and Master of Science degrees in Clinical Psychology. Dr. Villani is committed to enhancing clinical services, providing supervision and oversight to the clinical team, developing training opportunities, and working collaboratively with leadership to identify growth opportunities.

Dr. Richard Yudell

Psychiatrist

Dr. Richard Yudell is a licensed psychiatrist with 41 years of experience. His practice caters to teens and adults with a variety of issues including anxiety disorders, mood disorders, psychotic disorders, personality disorders, attention deficit hyperactivity disorder (ADHD), obsessive compulsive disorder (OCD), post traumatic-stress disorder (PTSD), and impulse control disorders.

Dawn DeBrocco

Chief Clinical Development & Implementation

Dawn has over 10 years of experience working with children, adolescents, and adults with a variety of complex needs. She has served in inpatient and outpatient medical and psychiatric settings, providing individual, group, and family therapy.
